The Realm of REDACTED; In the Beginning


In the beginning, REDACTED was a world like any other, however this was destined to change when calamity struck nearly causing an apocalyptical event. The only way to prevent this was the System. This System is named Yggdrasil and was put in place at the core of the world to save it, so that those that inhabit it and those that come to inhabit it can all live and thrive. As the years became decades became centuries, the old civilization came to an end as the world came to a soft reset, the old buildings being buried or lost to the sands of time. The only remnant now is the Labyrinth, a planet wide dungeon. As elvin life has gone on, they were joined by humans, dwarves, and other races that came to inhabit REDACTED. REDACTED's main continent is Sladridor, named for the old elvin kingdom that once encompassed the continent. The Elves of Sladridor came before all other races were introduced to REDACTED, in fact the Kingdom of Sladridor was taken down by an alliance of humans, dwarves and some others seeking fair and equal treatment, after which the alliance bided Sladridor into smaller kingdoms. The only remaining of those original kingdoms being that of Dwarga-ur, the dwarven kingdom under the Spine'ed Mountains.
