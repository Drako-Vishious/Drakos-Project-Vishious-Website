Series Genres

Series Premise


In this series of books the main character, Drako Morgolen, must accept and cope with the fact that he has been reincarnated from Earth to a fantasy, game-like world. During his new life he will meet other reincarnations including Hati, a pet earth wyrm(dragon) that evolves into a small silver frost wyrm upon making a familiar pact with Drako. Together they must not only survive but thrive in this new and exciting world.


Elevator Pitch

Drako Morgolen had been reborn, and in this new world he will find flames old and new. Will the flames sustain him or will he burn?
